Welcome to the Brighton & Hove Enviromental Partnership web platform!

We exist inorder to help Brighton & Hove residents to become more climate friendly. The Brighton and Hove city council is helping out by helping fund us as part of their climate insisative.

At the moment Wind is the most used fuel source at 29.4% in order to generate electricity, this is amazing as this is a renewable energy source meaning there is zero emissions generated . Also Gas is the second most used fuel source at 25.9% which is quite bad as it is a fossil fuel energy source that releases lots of emmisions, but in this time of a climate crisis we dramaticaly need to reduce the use of fossil fuels and use more zero emmision sources.
