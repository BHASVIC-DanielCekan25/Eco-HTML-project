Welcome to the Brighton & Hove Enviromental Partnership web platform!

We exist inorder to help Brighton & Hove residents to become more climate friendly. The Brighton and Hove city council is helping out by helping fund us as part of their climate insisative.

At the moment Gas is the most used fuel source at 32% in order to generate electricity, this is bad as it is a fossil fuel. Also wind is the second most used fuel source at 29.4% which is great as it is a renewable energy source, but in this time of a climate crisis we dramaticaly need to reduce the use of fossil fuels and use more zero emmision sources.
