Here are some tips to being more ecofriendly :

  • Use more of the local public transport system in order to get to places around the city.
  • Avoid single use products when possible
  • Remember to recycle or bin litter to avoid waste going into the sea or the nature
  • Once needed to upgrade to Energy Efficient Appliances if you haven't already
  • When washing clothes wash on the cold water setting this even speeds up the process!
  • Switch energy suppliers to one that produces their electricity with renewable sources
  • Before leaving home unplug your devices from the wall
  • 'Reduce, Reuse, Recycle'
